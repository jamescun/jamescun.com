I'm James, an engineer based in London. Working primarily for startups, I have been able to experience many diverse roles. In particular software engineering, site reliability engineering and cyber security. I primarily write Go.

I was one of the youngest founders to go through Y Combinator, as Chief Technology Officer of GoScale in the Summer 2012 batch. I additionally founded an exascale data analysis startup Hash Intelligence (pre-pivot).

I have released several open source projects, including WG-API, a JSON-RPC API for Wireguard, and Switcher, a daemon to run HTTP and SSH on the same port.

I currently work at Risk Ledger, but I have also worked at some of the most well known startups in London, such as Cuvva, Pusher and GoCardless.
